# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-01-11

BookImager (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy.  
This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 User-Provided Content
The App allows users to generate images based on text they provide (for example, book pages or excerpts).

- Text provided by the user is processed **only in real time** to generate images.
- We **do not store, retain, or profile** user-provided text.
- Generated images are not stored on our servers unless explicitly required for the requested operation.

---

### 1.2 Purchases and Subscriptions
The App offers optional paid subscriptions.

Subscription processing is handled by Apple’s App Store and a third-party service provider, **RevenueCat**.

The following data may be processed:
- Anonymous app user identifiers
- Subscription status
- Purchase and renewal information

This information is used **only** for:
- Enabling paid features
- Managing subscriptions
- Restoring purchases

We do not use payment information for advertising or tracking purposes.

---

### 1.3 Analytics and Diagnostics
We may collect limited technical information, such as:
- App version
- Device type
- Crash or error logs

This data is used solely to improve app stability and performance.

---

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect
We do **not** collect:
- Precise location data
- Contacts
- Photos or media libraries
- Audio recordings
- Messages
- Browsing history
- Personal identifiers such as name or email address

---

## 3. How We Use Information
Information is used only to:
- Provide core app functionality
- Enable and manage subscriptions
- Improve app reliability and performance

We do **not** use collected data for:
- Advertising
- Data brokerage
- Cross-app or cross-website tracking

---

## 4. Data Sharing
We do not sell or share personal data with third parties, except as necessary to operate the App (for example, subscription processing via Apple and RevenueCat).

All third-party services used are required to comply with applicable privacy laws.

---

## 5. Data Retention
- User-provided content is processed transiently and not retained.
- Subscription-related data is retained only as required to manage active subscriptions and comply with legal obligations.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy
The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 7. Security
We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this document.
